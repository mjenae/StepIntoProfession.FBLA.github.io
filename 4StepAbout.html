About the Platform

Step Into Profession is a web-based content project dedicated to guiding users of all ages down a path to success. Specifically, the platform offers snippets of advice to users on selecting and obtaining the job of his/her choice. The mission of the platform is to guide future businessmen and women down a pathway dedicated to ensuring his or her success.

Technical Organization of SIP

The main component of the SIP website is parallax, an effect in which the different background image movement speeds vary to create an illusionary depth during play. The effect brings about a much newer and more interactive level of online viewing while remaining organized, functional and appealing. At the same time, a fixed navigation bar is incorporated where, as the user scrolls further down the page, the navigation bar remains at the top of the page within view and reach, allowing access to other pages much swifter and easier.

Founder
SIP was founded by Madison Saunders (4th from the right) in December of 2017. Saunders was a junior in high school completely interested in coding and web design following her experience in the 2017 Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. A way in which she achieved her dreams of developing a helpful online tool to assist the general public included her partnership with the Future Business Leaders of America, an organization dedicated to merging business and education into a positive working relationship through innovative leadership and career development programs. Saunders, having been apart of the organization since her sophomore year, mixed vast amounts of research with her own knowledge learned as a (still) dedicated member and the result was Step Into Profession, denoted as a tool dedicated to outlining the path towards premiere excellence amongst future working class citizens.
